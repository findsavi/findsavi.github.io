SAVI Guess My Number Game Instructions:

First you have to guess n, where n is randomly generated number falls in between 1 and 99. If your guess is wrong addition then division operation will be done between n and guess so that the resultant (quotient) always falls in between 1 and 99. And least possible natural number is chosen as divisor to perform division. Eg: n = 40, guess=5 Addition I: (n+guess)=(40+5)=45 Now 45 can be divided with 3 or 5 or 9 or 15 or 45. System choose least possible divisor to divide 45. Division II: (n+guess)/least possible divisor in natural numbers. (45)/3=15 15 is the resultant that you have to guess. If you wrongly guess the resultant as 10. Again addition then division operation will be done like below Addition I: 15+10=25 Division II: 25/5=5 Now 5 is the resultant that you have to find out. If you got resultant you can find the n by substituting in the equation. Hint: The number you have to find out whether it is n or resultant always falls in between 1 and 99.
